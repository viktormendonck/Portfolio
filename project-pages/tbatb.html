The Boy and the Brush

This game is the result of a game jam with the theme: "It Spreads."

When thinking about "it spreads", we came up with the idea of spreading color into a grayed-out world.
This, combined with a platformer-style game, became the core concept.

In this project, my role involved handling all the movement and gameplay mechanics, while the other programmer focused on the shader required for coloring the world.

The biggest challenge in a game like this was figuring out how to clearly distinguish which areas were safe to land on and which weren’t.
The solution was to make something distinct in both grayscale and saturated colors, but in our case, we didn’t quite succeed.
