Posseidon's Moving Company

You know what isn't easy? Moving out.

You know what's hard? Doing it underwater as a shark.

But do you know what's even harder? Working for Poseidon's Moving Company (Pomoco™) and having to do all of that, as a shark, under way too much time pressure. What are you gonna do? Grab the furniture with your hands?

My first start-to-finish game that I made with some friends and strangers.
My best friend convinced me to try Godot as a game engine, which was nicer than expected.

The main thing I ended up learning from this project was the concept of "Scope Creep", because who would have thunk that trying to generate the levels completely randomly would be difficult.
This really showed me that a time limit is actually a thing, before this I could always just "put in more hours" so getting a constraint like that was an eye opening experience.
