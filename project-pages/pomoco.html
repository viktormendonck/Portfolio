Poseidon's Moving Company

Moving out is tough.

Doing it underwater as a shark? That’s even harder.

But you know what’s even more challenging? Working for Poseidon's Moving Company (Pomoco™) and having to juggle all of that — as a shark — under intense time pressure. What are you gonna do? Grab the furniture with your hands?

This was my first start-to-finish game, created with a mix of friends and strangers.
My best friend convinced me to try Godot as the game engine, and it turned out to be much nicer than I expected.

The most important lesson I learned from this project was the concept of "Scope Creep." Who would’ve thought that generating levels randomly would be so challenging?
It also taught me the reality of working under a time limit. Before this, I could always “put in more hours”, but facing an actual deadline was an eye-opening experience.
