Vulkan Modeler

Whilst studying at DAE we had a course called Graphics Programming 2, here we had an entire semester to make a Graphics Engine that we could render 3d objects and light in.

I ended up deciding to make a very simple 3d modeler. all it was able to do was selecting vertices by clicking on them and moving them with buttons on the keyboard.

The project itself was not my proudest achievement but I'm still proud of the graphics engine part
